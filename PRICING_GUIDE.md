## 💰 Pricing Rationale

### Factors Considered:
1. **Hardware Cost**: Initial investment and depreciation
2. **Power Consumption**: Electricity costs (GPUs consume 300-700W)
3. **Network Bandwidth**: Data transfer costs
4. **Maintenance**: Cooling, repairs, and uptime guarantees
5. **Market Rates**: Competitive with AWS, GCP, and Lambda Labs
6. **Monad Ecosystem**: Lower gas fees enable competitive pricing

### Comparison to Traditional Pricing:
- Traditional cloud GPU rentals: $1.50 - $5.00/hour (USD)
- Monad blockchain benefits: Lower transaction costs
- **MON Token Rate Assumption**: ~$0.15 USD per MON token

With this assumption:
- RTX 4090: 12 MON ≈ $1.80 USD (vs $2.50 market)
- A100: 28 MON ≈ $4.20 USD (vs $3.80 market)
- H100: 55 MON ≈ $8.25 USD (vs $10+ market)
