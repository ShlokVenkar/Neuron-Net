# ⚡ Neuron Net
### The Cosmic Compute Engine on Monad

> *"Cloud giants won't like this."*

**Neuron Net** is a decentralized GPU compute marketplace built on Monad Blockchain. Idle GPUs become revenue-generating assets for providers, while developers, students, and creators get instant, affordable compute power — without relying on AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure.

---

## 🚀 The Problem

GPU compute is the new oil. But right now, AWS, Google, and Azure control all of it. Expensive. Centralized. And completely out of reach for students and independent developers.

Every time we needed GPU power for our AI projects, cloud giants laughed at our bank accounts. So we stopped complaining and built our own solution.

---

## 💡 The Solution

Neuron Net is a three-sided marketplace connecting:

- **GPU Providers** — List your idle GPU and earn passively
- **Developers & Creators** — Rent affordable compute power instantly
- **Our In-House Team** — Don't know how to use a GPU? We do the work for you

---

## 🛠️ Three Ways to Use Neuron Net

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 🖥️ Self-Serve | Rent a GPU and access it remotely via AnyDesk |
| 👨‍💻 Hire a Freelancer | Post your task, hire an expert from our platform |
| 🤝 In-House Team | Let our dedicated team handle your compute task end-to-end |

---

## 🔗 Why Monad?

Monad's parallel execution and 10,000 TPS makes real-time GPU job matching and micropayments viable at scale. On Ethereum this would cost a fortune and take minutes. On Monad it's instant and cheap.

- ⚡ **<5ms Latency**
- 🔒 **Trustless Payments via Smart Contracts**
- 💸 **Zero Middlemen — Escrow Auto-Released on Job Completion**
- 🔄 **Real-time Job Matching at Scale**

## 🌟 Features

- 🔌 Wallet connect on Monad Testnet
- 📋 Browse GPU listings with specs, pricing, and availability
- 💳 Pay per hour or subscribe monthly
- 🤖 AI/ML training, video rendering, data processing support
- 👥 Freelancer marketplace for compute tasks
- 🏢 In-house team as service fallback
- ✅ On-chain job verification and payment settlement

---

## 💰 Business Model

- **15% platform commission** on every marketplace transaction (auto-collected via smart contract)
- **Service fees** from in-house team jobs
- **Subscription plans** for regular users
- **Priority listing fees** for GPU providers
- **Enterprise compute packages** for startups and AI companies
